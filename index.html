Bridge education foundation is one of the well-established and esteemed educational consultancy based in the heart of Kathmandu, Nepal. We help students to pursue higher education overseas. Motivated by our core values, ethics, expertise and leadership, we are one of the most trusted name in the field of overseas education. Facilitating best educational opportunities in the prestigious universities abroad, we rightly set the academic goals for aspiring students. Committed to the objectives of integrity and excellence, Bridge is an acknowledged leader in overseas educational consultancy in Kathmandu, Nepal. Bridge education foundation provides in-depth services and guidelines from the beginning to the end of the study process. We understand the initial decisions and processes of studying abroad can be challenging on student and their families. BEF strive to make these steps and transitions of studying abroad as smooth as possible through an interactive counselling process with students and their guardians. We offer students a choice of thousands of courses at various leading educational institutions in USA, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, UK, Ireland, India, Germany, Sweden, Denmark, Spain, Italy, Austria, Cyprus, and many other European countries.
